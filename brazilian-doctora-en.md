## My Life Before Islam

A Brazilian doctor who spent 15 years searching for the truth. She read the Qur’an specifically looking for mistakes in order to prove it false. She used to criticize Islam, yet she found within it logic and profound signs of divine inimitability.
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J7mkKFFjPQw)

## The Moment of Guidance

She was deeply affected by verses of the Qur’an that changed her perspective completely. While reading, she felt an unprecedented warmth and inner peace. She announced her acceptance of Islam via Twitter after realizing that it is the true religion.
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=13E5WG0VfPs)

## Reflections

She began calling others to Islam, emphasizing that the Qur’an contains powerful arguments that align with human nature. Her story shows how Allah guides those who sincerely seek the truth.
