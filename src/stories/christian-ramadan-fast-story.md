### Life Before Islam

Amin, a Dutch man, was a Christian from the Netherlands. He decided to try fasting during Ramadan in order to understand Islam from the inside, despite not having embraced it at the time. He grew up distant from Islam, yet he felt a strong curiosity about Muslim practices.
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xSvla3dAVZA)

### The Moment of Guidance

While fasting, he experienced deep inner peace and met Muslims who showed him great kindness, which led to an emotional acceptance of Islam. The Qur'an and the spiritual atmosphere of Ramadan completely transformed his heart.
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xSvla3dAVZA)

### Reflections

After embracing Islam, he encouraged others to experience Islam in a practical way, emphasizing that personal experience opens hearts. His story, featured in the "New Muslims" series, inspires Western audiences to explore Islam.
