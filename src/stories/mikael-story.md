## My Life Before Islam

Mickael was born and raised in France to a family of Italian heritage. He grew up in a devout Catholic household and was highly committed to his faith, regularly attending church and practicing religious rituals. Despite his commitment, he felt a spiritual void and had many unanswered questions regarding certain doctrines and the nature of God as described in the scriptures he was studying at the time.

## The Moment of Guidance

His path changed through meaningful discussions with Muslim friends. A significant turning point occurred when he compared the concept of creation in the Bible and the Quran; specifically, the idea of God resting after creation versus the Quranic verse stating that exhaustion never touches the Creator. After listening to the recitation of Surah Ar-Rahman and finding logical answers to his long-standing questions, he decided to embrace Islam at the age of 17.

## Reflections

Mickael reflects on the early challenges of practicing his faith in secret to avoid being ostracized by his family. He views Islam as a profound blessing that brought peace to his life and expresses a deep appreciation for the Quran as a guide. He often prays for his parents to understand his journey and find the same light, while also encouraging young people to seek knowledge and spiritual truth early in life.
