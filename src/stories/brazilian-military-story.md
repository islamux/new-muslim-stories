### My Life Before Islam

A Brazilian soldier who served in the army, distant from Islam and uninterested in religion. He lived an ordinary life filled with doubts about religions.
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4kHdx6Yl7gw)

### The Moment of Guidance

He was influenced by the story of a Brazilian person embracing Islam or by a Qur'anic miracle, which pushed him to research and eventually believe. He openly declared his Islam and began sharing it with others.
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=13E5WG0VfPs)

### Reflections

He began calling his fellow soldiers and his community to Islam, emphasizing the importance of practical, lived da'wah. His story highlights how Islam transforms individuals within non-Muslim societies.
[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4kHdx6Yl7gw)
