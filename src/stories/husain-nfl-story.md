## My Life Before Islam

* **Upbringing & Environment:** Husain grew up in the United States in an environment that encouraged sports and discipline. His father, who came from a tough background, taught his children how to transform their lives through hard work and resilience, which planted a spirit of challenge in Husain.

* **Athletic Success:** Thanks to his physical skills and speed, Husain achieved a dream that very few people on earth reach: playing in the National Football League (NFL). He played for the Minnesota Vikings and the Kansas City Chiefs.

* **Searching for Meaning:** Despite the fame, money, and massive athletic success, Husain always felt a need for spiritual balance. Amidst the roar of the stadiums and the bright lights, he sought a deeper connection with his Creator.

## The Moment of Guidance

* **Parental Influence:** Husain was raised with a love for the Quran from a young age due to his mother's influence. However, the major turning point was his realization that faith must be the true driver of his life, not just a set of inherited customs.

* **The Verse from Surah Ad-Duha:** Husain mentions that the verse "And He found you lost and guided [you]" (93:7) had a profound impact on his heart. He felt as if God was speaking to him personally, acknowledging his struggles and granting him guidance and peace.

* **Sacrifice for Hajj:** At the peak of his career, Husain made the courageous decision to leave the NFL for an entire year to perform Hajj with his family. This meant sacrificing multi-million dollar contracts and potentially losing his professional future, but he chose pleasing God over worldly gains.

* **The Stadium Prostration:** Upon returning to the league, Husain scored a crucial touchdown and immediately performed *Sajdah* (prostration) in the middle of the field. This spontaneous act of gratitude sparked a massive media buzz, introducing millions to his identity as a Muslim athlete.

## Reflections

* **The Silent Dawah:** Husain believes the best way to call others to Islam is through character. He is known for his constant smile, avoidance of foul language, and steadfastness in prayer—especially Fajr—even during grueling training schedules.

* **Raising Children:** He is dedicated to teaching his children the Quran and connecting them to the mosque, believing that a family raised on faith is the foundation of a healthy society.

* **Commitment in Ramadan:** Husain became famous for fasting during Ramadan while competing in the physically demanding NFL. This earned him the respect of his teammates and the American public, who were amazed by his endurance and religious discipline.

* **The True Message:** Husain believes that real success is not measured by touchdowns or wealth, but by living as a true Muslim who represents his faith in the best possible light to the world.
