## My Life Before Islam
Abdurrahman, an Italian man, grew up questioning Christianity from a young age and became an atheist at 16, rejecting the idea of a Creator. For 33 years, he believed in evolution and the Big Bang as explanations for the universe, engaging in a lifestyle of clubbing and alcohol on weekends. [hadithoftheday](https://hadithoftheday.com/abdurrahman-story-of-an-italian-who-found-islam-in-the-maldives/)

## The Moment of Guidance
A visit to a mosque in the Maldives changed everything; he learned about the Quran as an unaltered divine book with scientific miracles. He was deeply moved by a Quranic verse and the Basmala, realizing Jesus (Isa) is a prophet, not God, leading him to pronounce the Shahada on November 26, 2014. [forevermuslim](https://forevermuslim.in/4668/)

## Reflection
He faced family rejection and severed ties with friends, removing alcohol and images from his home to align with Islamic principles. Despite language barriers, he learned prayer via YouTube and books, finding inner peace and regularly visiting the mosque, sharing his story in programs like "Guided by the Quran."
